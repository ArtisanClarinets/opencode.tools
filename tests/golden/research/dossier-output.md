# Research Dossier Template: Acme Corp

## Client Summary
Acme Corp is a fictional B2B SaaS company that provides specialized project management tools for the construction industry. Their core product focuses on real-time material tracking and compliance reporting, primarily serving small to mid-sized contractors. Their ambition is to leverage AI for process optimization.

## Competitive Landscape

| Competitor | Primary Differentiation |
| :--- | :--- |
| **BuildTools** | Strongest market share in the mid-size contractor segment. |
| **ConstractPro** | Low-cost solution, but limited mobile functionality. |
| **PlanSwift** | Highly customizable reporting but requires significant setup. |
| **Fieldwire** | Excellent mobile/field experience, but lacks compliance focus. |
| **Procore (Enterprise)** | Dominates the large enterprise space; high price point. |

## Technical Analysis

### Constraints (3 identified)
1. **Legacy Dependence**: Existing client base relies on legacy, on-premise solutions that require complex data migration strategies.
2. **Regulatory Compliance**: High regulatory requirements in the target market (compliance reporting and auditing is a critical, non-negotiable feature).
3. **Connectivity Issues**: Mobile app performance in low-connectivity construction zones is a known issue, hindering real-time tracking goals.

### Opportunities (3 identified)
1. **AI Waste Prediction**: Implementing an AI-driven material waste prediction feature to give clients a competitive edge in cost reduction.
2. **Drone Integration**: Integration with drone imaging platforms to automate site progress monitoring and link visual data to material tracking.
3. **European Market Expansion**: Adapting the compliance module to meet European regulatory standards for future market entry.
